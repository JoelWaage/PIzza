Our Specialty Pies


  • P-Egg-STO
    • Our signature pie with house-made pesto as the sauce, a light sprinkling of hard cheeses all topped with an egg, sunnyside up. (If you're getting this one delivered, we recommend cooking the egg on the stovetop before serving).

  • Tableside
    • Tableside is a reference to our favorite appetizer at any Mexican restaurant, the tableside guacamole. Tableside has an ultra this crust pizza, topped with a Tapatio inspired red sauce and cotija cheese. We top off the hot pie with a heaping scoop of our fresh guacamole

  • Buffalo buffalo Buffalo buffalo buffalo buffalo buffalo Buffalo buffalo
    • This hearty pie comes with our house-made signature red sauce, topped with a healthy portion of ground bison meat spiced to perfection. Add some mozzerella and a smattering of bleu cheese, and you have a delicious pizza inspired by Noam Chomsky's baffling, yet gramatically correct sentence.
